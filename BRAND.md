# MASE Brand Guide

## Identity
**Name:** MASE Services  
**Tagline:** The McKinsey of AI  
**Position:** Premier AI consulting, research, and education for legacy businesses

## Logo Mark
The MASE logo is a stylized "M" formed by ascending data points/nodes connected by lines, suggesting both growth trajectory and network intelligence. Minimal, geometric, tech-forward.

**Primary lockup:** MASE_ (with cyan underscore cursor)
**Icon only:** The M mark (for favicons, social)

## Colors

### Primary
- **Cyan Accent:** #00f3ff (the signature "intelligence" color)
- **Deep Black:** #030305 (background)
- **Pure White:** #ffffff (text, buttons)

### Secondary  
- **Purple (Strategy):** #a78bfa
- **Green (Implementation):** #4ade80
- **Amber (Industry):** #f59e0b
- **Gray (Text):** #9ca3af

### Usage
- Cyan for highlights, accents, links, important callouts
- Purple for strategy/planning content
- Green for implementation/action content
- Amber for industry-specific content
- Never use emojis (Lucide icons only)

## Typography
- **Headlines:** Space Grotesk (bold, modern, geometric)
- **Body:** Inter (clean, readable)
- **Code/Technical:** Monospace

## Visual Style

### Infographics — CITATION-WORTHY STYLE
Goal: Clean enough to be cited by HBR, McKinsey, academic papers. No branding.

**Do:**
- White or very light gray background (#ffffff or #f8f9fa)
- Simple, clean lines and shapes
- Standard business colors (blue, gray, green, orange for contrast)
- Clear hierarchy: title → main content → source
- Minimal design focused 100% on explaining the concept
- Include "Source: [data source]" at bottom when citing stats
- Sans-serif fonts (Inter, Helvetica, Arial)

**Don't:**
- No MASE logos or branding on infographics
- No neon/glow effects
- No sci-fi aesthetics
- No dark backgrounds (harder to print/embed)
- No decorative elements that don't serve the explanation

### Charts & Graphs
- Light background for printability
- Standard colors: blue (#2563eb), green (#16a34a), orange (#ea580c), gray (#6b7280)
- Clear axis labels
- Data labels directly on charts when possible
- Source citation at bottom

### Photography Style
- If using photos: dark, moody, tech-forward
- Prefer abstract/geometric over stock photos
- Light trails, data visualizations, cityscapes at night

## Iconography
- Lucide icons only (no emojis)
- Stroke weight: 1.5-2px
- Color: cyan accent or white/gray

## Voice & Tone
- Direct, confident, not arrogant
- Data-driven, cite sources
- Accessible but not dumbed-down
- "Here's what the data says" not "We think"

## File Naming
Images: `mase-{type}-{name}-{date}.png`
- `mase-infographic-ai-adoption-2026-02-07.png`
- `mase-chart-benchmark-results-2026-02-07.png`
- `mase-icon-favicon-32.png`

## Favicon Specs
- 32x32, 180x180 (Apple touch), 512x512 (PWA)
- The M mark in cyan on transparent or dark background
- Simple, recognizable at small sizes
