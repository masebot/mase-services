Questions from Legal Leaders

Is this secure enough for client-privileged documents?

Absolutely. We deploy solutions that keep data within your infrastructure or use enterprise-grade AI with SOC 2 compliance, encryption at rest and in transit, and no model training on your data. Attorney-client privilege is non-negotiable.

How do you handle different practice areas?

We customize AI models to your specific practice. An M&A contract review system differs from a litigation document review system. We train on your precedents and your firm's standards.

Will this replace our associates?

No‚Äîit makes them dramatically more effective. Associates shift from reading every word to reviewing AI-generated summaries and focusing on judgment calls. They become more valuable, not obsolete. Firms that implement AI typically take on more work, not less staff.

What about hallucinations and AI errors?

We build human-in-the-loop systems where AI augments attorney review, not replaces it. All AI output is reviewed by attorneys before client delivery. The AI handles the tedium; your lawyers handle the judgment.

Most firms see measurable impact within 30 days. We start with one high-value workflow, prove ROI, then expand. No 18-month implementation timelines.
